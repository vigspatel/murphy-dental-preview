## A Specialized Solution for Deep Dental Infection

When decay or trauma reaches the innermost part of the tooth, the pulp, it can cause intense sensitivity, swelling, and persistent discomfort. Root canal therapy is a highly effective restorative procedure designed to remove the infected tissue while preserving the external structure of your tooth.

By cleaning and disinfecting the internal canals, we stop the spread of infection and prevent the need for a total tooth extraction. Far from the "painful" reputation it once had, modern endodontic care is a routine way to stabilize your bite and maintain the natural alignment of your jaw, ensuring your smile remains functional and intact for years to come.

## The Procedure

At Murphy Dental Center, we understand that the prospect of a root canal can be intimidating, which is why we emphasize a stress-free environment and complete local anesthesia to ensure the process is as painless as a standard filling. Dr. Bulsara utilizes high-definition digital imaging to map out the unique anatomy of your tooth’s roots, allowing for a more predictable and efficient procedure.

Once the infection is cleared and the canal is sealed with a bio-compatible material, we often recommend a custom-made crown frequently available via our same-day technology to reinforce and protect the treated tooth. Our high-integrity approach ensures that you leave our Murphy, NC office not only free from pain but with a durable, healthy restoration that blends seamlessly with your smile.
