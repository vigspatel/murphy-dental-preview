## Comprehensive Protection for Compromised Teeth

A dental crown, often referred to as a "cap," is a versatile restoration that fully encases a tooth that has been weakened by extensive decay, large fractures, or a root canal procedure. Unlike a filling that sits inside a tooth, a crown provides a complete 360-degree shield, absorbing the pressure of chewing and preventing further structural breakdown.

At Murphy Dental Center, we prioritize materials that offer both biocompatibility and aesthetic excellence. By utilizing high-quality porcelain or ceramic, we can replicate the light-reflecting properties of your natural enamel, ensuring your restoration is virtually indistinguishable from your surrounding teeth while offering the structural integrity needed for daily function.

## Precision Restoration

Our practice integrates the latest breakthroughs in dental engineering to provide a restoration experience that respects both your health and your schedule. Dr. Bulsara utilizes sophisticated intraoral cameras and on-site fabrication tools to eliminate the need for traditional, goopy molds and the typical fourteen-day lag associated with outside labs. By capturing a high-resolution 3D blueprint of your mouth, we can design a prosthetic that aligns flawlessly with your natural occlusion, preventing future issues like muscle tension or uneven wear.

Whether you require a "cap" to reinforce a fractured molar or to complete an aesthetic upgrade, our Murphy, NC specialists deliver a refined, durable result that mimics the strength of your original tooth.
