## Bridging the Gap for Enhanced Oral Stability

When a tooth is lost, the surrounding teeth naturally begin to tilt or shift into the empty space, which can lead to bite misalignment, jaw pain, and an increased risk of gum disease. A dental bridge acts as a permanent anchor, utilizing the healthy teeth on either side of the gap to support a custom-crafted replacement tooth (a pontic).

This not only restores the visual completeness of your smile but also redistributes the workload of your bite, preventing excessive wear on your remaining natural teeth. By choosing a bridge at Murphy Dental Center, you are investing in a fixed solution that eliminates the slipping or discomfort often associated with removable appliances.

Our high-integrity approach ensures that your bridge is perfectly balanced for a comfortable chew and a seamless look.
