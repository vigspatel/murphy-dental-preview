## A Modern Approach to Cavity Repair

When a tooth is compromised by a cavity, timely intervention is key to stopping the spread of decay and avoiding more invasive procedures like root canals or extractions. At Murphy Dental Center, we have moved beyond the "silver" (amalgam) fillings of the past, opting instead for advanced composite resins.

These materials are not only aesthetically superior, as they are custom-shaded to match your tooth, but they also offer a superior bond. Because the resin chemically adheres to the tooth structure, it requires less removal of healthy enamel, allowing us to preserve more of your natural tooth while providing a seal that is highly resistant to temperature changes and further bacterial invasion.

## Your Restorative Visit at our Murphy Office

The comfort of our patients is the centerpiece of our restorative care. When you come in for a filling, Dr. Bulsara and our team utilize precise local anesthesia and gentle techniques to ensure the process is entirely painless. After the decay is carefully removed and the area is sterilized, the composite material is applied in layers and cured with a specialized light to achieve immediate strength.

We take great care to shape and polish the restoration to match the unique anatomy of your bite, ensuring that your tooth feels completely natural to the tongue and functions perfectly when chewing. Our high-integrity approach means you receive a durable, "invisible" repair that keeps your smile healthy and your confidence high.
