## Improving Tooth Structural Integrity

A chipped or cracked tooth can range from a minor cosmetic annoyance to a serious structural threat that exposes the sensitive inner nerves of your mouth. Even if a crack is not visible to the naked eye, it can allow bacteria to seep into the tooth’s core, leading to infection or further splitting. At Murphy Dental Center, we view every repair as an opportunity to reinforce your tooth's long-term durability.

By utilizing high-resolution diagnostic imaging, we can determine the depth of the damage and select the most conservative treatment whether it's a simple smoothing of a jagged edge, a composite bonding for a minor chip, or a full-coverage crown for more extensive structural fractures.

## From Arrival to Procedure

When you arrive at our practice with a damaged tooth, Dr. Bulsara conducts a comprehensive assessment to ensure your bite is correctly aligned and the tooth's root is secure. We pride ourselves on offering versatile solutions under one roof; for instance, many fractures can be resolved in a single visit using our advanced one-day crown technology, which eliminates the need for temporary restorations or multiple appointments.

Our high-integrity approach focuses on matching the repair perfectly to your natural tooth shade and texture, ensuring the final result is as strong as it is beautiful. From the moment you walk into our Murphy, NC office, our goal is to eliminate any sensitivity and return you to your daily life with a fully functional, flawless smile.
