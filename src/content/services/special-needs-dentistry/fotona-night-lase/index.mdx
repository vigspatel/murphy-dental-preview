## Revolutionizing Airway Stability Through Laser Energy

NightLase by Fotona is a breakthrough, patient-friendly procedure designed to address the physical vibrations that cause loud snoring and mild sleep apnea. Rather than cutting tissue or forcing air into the lungs, this system utilizes gentle, superficial laser light to warm the oral mucosa at the back of the throat.

This precise thermal energy triggers a natural contraction of collagen, effectively "firming up" the soft palate and surrounding tissues that typically sag and obstruct the airway during sleep. By increasing the structural tension of these areas, the airway remains wider and more stable, significantly reducing the frequency and volume of snoring for a more peaceful night.
