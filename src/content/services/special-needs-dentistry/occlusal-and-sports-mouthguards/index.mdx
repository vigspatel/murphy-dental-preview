## Prioritizing Oral Health and Patient Comfort

Whether you are an athlete on the field or a "heavy grinder" in your sleep, your teeth face constant structural threats that standard over-the-counter options simply can't handle. Occlusal guards often called nightguards serve as a shock-absorbing buffer that prevents the flat-level wear and enamel fractures associated with bruxism, effectively silencing the strain on your temporomandibular joints.

On the other hand, our high-impact sports guards are engineered to dissipate the energy of a sudden blow, significantly lowering the risk of tooth loss or soft tissue lacerations. By moving away from "boil-and-bite" generic versions which are often bulky and interfere with oxygen intake we provide a streamlined defense that stays locked in place when it matters most.

## Precision Mapping for Lasting Protection

When you visit us in Murphy, NC for a protective appliance, we prioritize a fit so exact that it feels like a natural extension of your teeth. Dr. Bulsara utilizes high-definition intraoral imaging to capture every groove and contour of your bite, allowing for the fabrication of a guard that aligns perfectly with your opposing teeth without causing jaw fatigue.

This level of customization ensures that the material whether it’s a dense acrylic for nighttime protection or a multi-layered laminate for athletics remains comfortable and durable over years of use. At Murphy Dental Center, we view a custom guard as a vital insurance policy for your smile, helping you avoid the complex and expensive restorative work that follows a major dental injury or chronic tooth-on-tooth friction.
