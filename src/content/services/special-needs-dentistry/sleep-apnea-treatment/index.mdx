## Restoring Airway Flow Without the Mask

Obstructive Sleep Apnea (OSA) occurs when the soft tissues at the back of the throat collapse during sleep, physically blocking the passage of oxygen and causing the brain to wake the body repeatedly throughout the night. Instead of relying on a loud, restrictive CPAP machine, our practice provides Mandibular Advancement Devices specialized, professional-grade mouthpieces worn only during sleep.

These custom-engineered appliances gently shift the lower jaw into a forward position, which naturally tightens the soft tissues and prevents the tongue from obstructing the throat. By maintaining a clear, consistent airway, these devices significantly reduce snoring and ensure your vital organs receive the oxygen required for a truly restorative night's rest.

## A Personalized Path to Quiet, Restful Nights

Your journey to better sleep at Murphy Dental Center begins with a precise evaluation of your oral anatomy and a review of your diagnostic sleep study. Dr. Bulsara utilizes digital impression technology to create a high-resolution 3D map of your teeth, ensuring your appliance fits securely and comfortably without the need for traditional, "goopy" molds.

We focus on a collaborative care model, often coordinating with your primary physician or sleep specialist to ensure your treatment effectively manages your specific apnea severity. Each device we provide is adjustable, discreet, and travel-friendly, offering a highly effective solution for our Murphy, NC patients who want to wake up feeling refreshed and alert without the burden of hoses or masks.
