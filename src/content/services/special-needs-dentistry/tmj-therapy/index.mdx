## Preventing Complications from Third Molar Impaction

Temporomandibular Joint (TMJ) disorders often stem from a mechanical imbalance between your teeth and the delicate hinges that connect your jaw to your skull. When this complex system of muscles and ligaments is out of sync, it can manifest as persistent earaches, restricted movement, or a grating sound during meals.

Our therapy focuses on decompressing the joint and soothing hyperactive facial muscles that have been overworked due to a misaligned bite or "bruxing" habits. By identifying the specific physiological stressors whether they are structural or muscular, we can implement a strategy that reduces inflammation and prevents the long-term degradation of the jaw’s cartilage.

## A Targeted Roadmap to Neuromuscular Relief

At Murphy Dental Center, Dr. Bulsara conducts a comprehensive analysis to map your jaw's natural "neutral" position and identify areas of interference. We frequently utilize custom-engineered orthotics highly specialized oral splints that are fundamentally different from standard nightguards. These medical-grade appliances are designed to subtly retrain your facial muscles and reposition the mandible, allowing the joint to rest and the surrounding nerves to decompress.

Our Murphy, NC team carefully monitors your progress, recalibrating your treatment to ensure your teeth meet in a way that eliminates muscle strain. This approach moves beyond temporary fixes, aiming instead to balance your oral-facial system for lasting, daily comfort.
