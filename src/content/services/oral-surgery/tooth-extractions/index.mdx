## Prioritizing Oral Health and Patient Comfort

While the goal is always to preserve natural teeth, there are circumstances where an extraction is the most effective path toward long-term wellness. Whether a tooth has been structurally compromised by deep fractures or is impacted beneath the gum line, removing the source of irritation prevents the spread of infection to the rest of the jaw.

At Murphy Dental Center, we utilize advanced diagnostic imaging to assess the root's position and its relationship to nearby nerves before we begin. This proactive planning ensures that the removal is as non-invasive as possible, maintaining the integrity of the surrounding bone and soft tissue to facilitate a faster, uncomplicated healing process.

## As Painless As Possible

When you visit Dr. Bulsara for a tooth removal, the experience is centered on a calm, stress-free environment and effective pain management. We employ modern local anesthetics to completely numb the area, ensuring that you feel only slight pressure rather than sharp sensations during the treatment.

Once the tooth is carefully luxated and removed, we provide detailed aftercare instructions and discuss customized replacement options such as implants or bridges to keep your bite aligned. Our clinical team in Murphy, NC, remains dedicated to your recovery, offering a supportive follow-up process to ensure the extraction site closes properly and your oral health is fully restored.
