## Restoring Structural Volume and Soft Tissue Hydration

As the skin ages, it loses the collagen and hyaluronic acid necessary to maintain a plump, elastic appearance, often leading to hollowed cheeks or pronounced folds around the mouth. Dermal fillers act as a specialized "volumizer," utilizing a gel-like substance to fill in these depleted areas from beneath the surface.

By strategically injecting these fillers into targeted zones such as the nasolabial folds or the vertical lines above the lips we can smooth out deep-set wrinkles and redefine facial contours. This treatment doesn't just "mask" age; it restores the three-dimensional architecture of the face, providing an immediate lift that complements the alignment and brightness of your teeth.
