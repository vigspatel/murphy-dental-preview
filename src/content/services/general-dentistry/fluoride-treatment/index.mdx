## The Science of Enamel Remineralization

Fluoride is a naturally occurring mineral that plays a critical role in oral health by attracting calcium and phosphate to your teeth. This process, known as remineralization, repairs weakened enamel before a cavity can even form.

At Murphy Dental Center, our concentrated fluoride treatments are significantly more effective than over-the-counter toothpastes, providing a high-potency barrier that neutralizes the acids produced by plaque and bacteria. Whether you are prone to frequent cavities or simply want to protect your investment in your dental health, fluoride acts as a foundational element of proactive, preventive care for patients of all ages.

## Your Fluoride Treatment Experience

When you visit Murphy Dental Center for a fluoride treatment, the process is fast, comfortable, and seamlessly integrated into your routine cleaning. Dr. Bulsara and our skilled hygiene team apply a specialized fluoride varnish or gel directly to the surfaces of your teeth using a small brush or a comfortable tray.

The treatment adheres quickly and begins working immediately to harden your enamel from the outside in. There are no needles, no discomfort, and no downtime just a simple, effective step toward a cavity-free future.

We tailor every application to your specific needs, ensuring you receive the optimal level of protection in a relaxing, high-integrity professional environment.
