## Restoring Form with Composite Artistry

Dental bonding is an ideal solution for those seeking immediate cosmetic improvements for chipped, cracked, or slightly decayed teeth. Using a pliable, tooth-colored composite resin, Dr. Bulsara can literally "sculpt" a new surface onto your natural tooth, masking flaws and reinforcing weakened structures.

This versatile treatment is particularly effective for filling small spaces between teeth or altering the length of a tooth to create a more uniform appearance. Because the resin is available in a vast array of shades, we can perfectly match the translucency and hue of your surrounding enamel, making the repair virtually indistinguishable from the rest of your smile.

## The Bonding Process at our Murphy Office

One of the greatest advantages of choosing bonding at Murphy Dental Center is the speed and simplicity of the appointment. Unlike crowns or veneers that require laboratory fabrication, bonding is performed entirely chairside, often without the need for local anesthesia.

After lightly etching the tooth surface to ensure a tight seal, the resin is applied, shaped by hand, and hardened instantly using a high-intensity curing light. We then polish the restoration to a natural luster that feels smooth to the tongue and looks brilliant under any light. It is a high-integrity, cost-effective way to achieve a "smile gallery" result in less than an hour per tooth.
