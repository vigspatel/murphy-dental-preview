## The Discreet Path to a Perfectly Aligned Smile

Invisalign® has revolutionized orthodontic care by replacing bulky metal brackets and wires with a series of custom-molded, BPA-free plastic trays. These aligners apply gentle, consistent pressure to shift your teeth into their ideal positions without interfering with your daily routine or oral hygiene. Because the trays are removable, you can maintain your regular diet and brush and floss with ease, significantly reducing the risk of staining or decay often seen with traditional braces.

This technology is highly effective for correcting crowding, gaps, and bite misalignments, offering a sophisticated solution for adults and teens who want a straighter smile without the "metal-mouth" aesthetic.

## Invisible Aligner

At Murphy Dental Center, your Invisalign® experience begins with a high-tech digital scan, no messy, uncomfortable putty impressions required. Dr. Bulsara uses these 3D models to map out the precise movement of every tooth, allowing you to see a virtual representation of your final results before you even begin your first set of trays.

Our team follows a high-integrity approach, monitoring your progress through periodic check-ins to ensure your treatment stays on track and remains comfortable. We prioritize efficiency and precision, providing you with a series of aligners that are uniquely yours, leading to a functional, beautiful result that integrates seamlessly into your life in Murphy, NC.
