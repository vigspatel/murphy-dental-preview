## Seamless Enamel Restoration

White spot lesions often caused by early-stage decay, fluorosis, or mineral loss during braces can make an otherwise healthy smile look uneven. These spots occur because porous enamel scatters light differently than the surrounding tooth structure. Icon Resin Infiltration works by deep-cleaning these microscopic pores and filling them with a specialized, light-curing resin.

This liquid resin mimics the optical properties of healthy enamel, effectively "fading" the white spots away from the inside out. Unlike traditional fillings, this method preserves every bit of your natural tooth, halting early decay in its tracks while creating a smooth, aesthetically pleasing surface.

## Micro-Invasive

Choosing Icon at our Murphy, NC office means a completely pain-free experience that requires no shots and no dental drills. Dr. Bulsara and our clinical team begin by gently preparing the surface of the affected teeth to open the enamel pores.

Once the tooth is ready, the high-penetration resin is applied and allowed to soak into the lesions before being hardened with a specialized curing light. The entire process is finished with a fine polish, leaving your teeth looking vibrant and consistent. It is a high-integrity, conservative alternative to more invasive cosmetic work, providing immediate results that blend perfectly with your natural smile.
