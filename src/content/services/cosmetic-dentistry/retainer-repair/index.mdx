## Preserving Your Orthodontic Success

A broken or ill-fitting retainer is more than just an inconvenience; it is a risk to the alignment you spent months or even years achieving. When a retainer cracks, loses its shape, or a bonded wire becomes detached, your teeth can begin to "relapse" or shift back toward their original positions almost immediately.

Our repair services focus on restoring the structural integrity and precise tension of your appliance to maintain the stability of your bite. By addressing these issues promptly, we help you avoid the need for corrective orthodontic treatment in the future, saving you time, discomfort, and expense while keeping your "smile gallery" results intact.

## Restoring Your Appliance at our Murphy Office

When you bring your damaged appliance to Murphy Dental Center, Dr. Bulsara and our technical team perform a detailed fit assessment to determine if a chairside repair is possible or if a digital scan for a replacement is required. Utilizing high-integrity materials and precision bonding techniques, we can often re-attach fixed retainers or smooth out rough edges on removable trays during a single brief visit.

If your retainer is beyond repair, we utilize our advanced 3D scanning technology to create a digital map of your current alignment, ensuring your new custom-fit retainer is more comfortable and durable than the original. Our goal is to get you back to your routine with a secure, reliable appliance that safeguards your smile’s future.
