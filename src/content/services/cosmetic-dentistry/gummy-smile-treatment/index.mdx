## Redefining Your Smile’s Proportions

Excessive gingival display often called a "gummy smile" can make teeth appear shorter than they actually are, even when they are perfectly healthy. This common aesthetic concern occurs when the gum tissue overpowers the tooth structure, creating an asymmetrical look.

At Murphy Dental Center, we view gum health as the "frame" for your teeth; by gently removing or repositioning small amounts of tissue, we can create a more pleasing ratio between your gums and enamel. This treatment isn't just about vanity, it can also make it easier to maintain oral hygiene by reducing deep pockets where bacteria tend to hide, ensuring your smile is as healthy as it is beautiful.

## The Path to a Balanced Gum Line

When you visit Dr. Bulsara to discuss a gummy smile, we begin with a detailed analysis of your "smile zone" to ensure the results look entirely natural. Using advanced technology, we perform a minimally invasive procedure often referred to as crown lengthening or gingival sculpting.

Unlike traditional methods that required extensive recovery, our modern approach prioritizes your comfort, typically requiring only local anesthesia and offering rapid healing times. We take a high-integrity, conservative approach to ensure we only remove what is necessary to achieve your goals. You’ll leave our Murphy, NC office with a refined, symmetrical appearance that highlights your teeth and boosts your confidence every time you laugh.
