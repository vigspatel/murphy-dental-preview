## Maximizing Bone Potential with Strategic Angling

Unlike standard implant procedures that might require a dozen individual posts, the All-on-4 method utilizes just four titanium cylinders to support an entire upper or lower arch. The genius of this approach lies in the posterior anchors, which are tilted at a specific 45-degree angle to bypass low-density areas and capitalize on the strongest parts of your jawbone.

This unique positioning often eliminates the need for expensive, time-consuming bone grafting and allows for a slimmer prosthetic that doesn't cover the roof of your mouth. It essentially acts as a permanent, high-strength bridge for your entire jaw, providing a level of biting force that traditional plates simply cannot match.

## Your Same-Day Transformation Experience

At our Murphy practice, we streamline the path to your new smile using high-precision 3D diagnostics to plan the exact geometry of your restoration before the surgery even begins. On the day of the procedure, any remaining compromised teeth are removed and the four anchors are placed; in many cases, a temporary set of teeth can be attached immediately so you never have to leave our office with a gap in your smile.

As your jaw heals and fuses with the titanium, we craft a final, durable prosthetic that is custom-weighted and shaded to mirror your ideal aesthetic. This comprehensive overhaul results in a functional, fixed solution that looks, feels, and acts like healthy natural teeth from the moment you leave Murphy Dental Center.
