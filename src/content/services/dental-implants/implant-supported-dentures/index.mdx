## Engineering a Permanent Oral Reconstruction

Unlike traditional prosthetics that rely on the soft tissues of the mouth for support, these restorations are anchored directly into the jawbone via a series of titanium posts. This structural integration allows the appliance to be screw-retained, meaning it stays in your mouth 24/7 just like natural teeth.

By removing the need for a bulky, palate-covering base, this design restores your ability to fully taste your favorite foods and feel the temperature of your drinks. It is a comprehensive mechanical replacement for an entire arch, designed for those who want to move past the limitations of traditional dentures and regain a bite force that can handle almost any diet.

## The Surgical and Prosthetic Integration

Transitioning to a fixed implant-supported bridge involves a sophisticated blend of surgical planning and custom craftsmanship. At our Murphy, NC practice, we utilize 3D cone-beam computed tomography (CBCT) to visualize your bone architecture in three dimensions, allowing for the strategic placement of anchors in areas of highest density.

Once these foundations have successfully fused with your jaw, we collaborate with you to design a prosthetic arch that complements your facial features and speech patterns. Our goal is to provide a seamless transition from tooth loss to a robust, functional smile that requires no adhesives and remains a permanent part of your daily life.
