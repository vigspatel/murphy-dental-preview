## Redefining Precision with Dual-Wavelength Laser Power

The Fotona LightWalker is a sophisticated medical tool that utilizes two distinct laser wavelengths to treat both hard and soft tissues with unmatched accuracy. By adjusting the frequency of the light, Dr. Bulsara can perform many traditional "drill and fill" procedures without the vibration, noise, or pressure associated with high-speed handpieces.

This technology is incredibly versatile, allowing us to disinfect root canals, treat periodontal disease, and perform precise gum contouring with microscopic precision. For many of our Murphy, NC patients, this means a dental experience that is virtually pain-free and significantly more relaxing than conventional mechanical methods.

## Biocompatible Healing and Accelerated Recovery

Beyond the immediate comfort during the procedure, the LightWalker system promotes a faster and more natural healing process. The laser’s energy naturally sterilizes the treatment site and bio-stimulates the surrounding tissue, which minimizes bleeding and reduces the risk of post-operative infection. Because the laser is so targeted, it preserves more of your healthy tooth structure and gum tissue compared to traditional surgical tools.

At Murphy Dental Center, we use this "light-touch" approach to deliver high-integrity results that prioritize your long-term wellness, ensuring that your recovery is as swift and uncomplicated as possible.
