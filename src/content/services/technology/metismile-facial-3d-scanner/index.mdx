## Syncing Your Smile with Your Facial Symmetry

The Metismile Facial 3D Scanner represents the next frontier in personalized restorative dentistry by capturing a high-resolution, three-dimensional record of your facial architecture. Unlike traditional methods that view the mouth in isolation, this advanced imaging allows our clinical team to analyze how your teeth interact with your lips, cheeks, and natural contours.

By recording your face in a digital space, we can evaluate the aesthetic balance of a proposed treatment from every possible angle. This ensures that the final result whether it is a set of porcelain veneers or a full-arch restoration is biologically and aesthetically harmonized with your specific facial proportions.

## The Future of Comprehensive Treatment Visualization

When you visit Murphy Dental Center, the scanning process is entirely non-invasive, involving no radiation and taking only about ten seconds to complete. Using sophisticated infrared and optical sensors, the Metismile scanner generates a lifelike digital avatar that serves as a blueprint for your dental transformation.

This allows Dr. Bulsara to collaborate with you during the planning phase, showing you a realistic simulation of your outcome before any work begins. By integrating this facial data with our design software, we eliminate the guesswork of reconstructive dentistry, providing our Murphy, NC patients with a predictable and high-precision path toward a rejuvenated appearance.
