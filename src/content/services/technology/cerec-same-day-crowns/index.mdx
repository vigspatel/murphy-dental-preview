## Streamlining the Restorative Journey via Digital Impressions

CEREC (Chairside Economical Restoration of Esthetic Ceramics) revolutionizes the traditional crown process by consolidating multiple clinical steps into a single, efficient session. Instead of the messy, uncomfortable silicone "goop" used for impressions in the past, we utilize a slender intraoral camera to capture a detailed 3D digital map of your tooth.

This high-resolution data allows our team to architect a custom-fit restoration that matches your unique bite and dental anatomy with pinpoint accuracy. By moving the laboratory work directly to your chairside, we eliminate the need for fragile temporary crowns and the inconvenient return trips usually required for restorative work.

## Precision Fabrication While You Wait

When you visit Murphy Dental Center for a same-day crown, you can watch as your permanent restoration is sculpted from a solid block of medical-grade ceramic in our on-site milling unit. Dr. Bulsara meticulously selects a porcelain shade and translucency that blends invisibly with your natural smile, ensuring the final result is as beautiful as it is durable.

Once the milling is complete, the crown is polished and bonded into place immediately, allowing you to walk out of our Murphy, NC office with a fully restored, functional tooth. This "high-integrity" approach not only respects your busy schedule but also ensures a superior fit, as the restoration is placed while the prepared tooth is at its healthiest state.
