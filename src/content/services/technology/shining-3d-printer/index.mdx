## Accelerating the Path to Restorative Success

The integration of the Shining 3D printing system at Murphy Dental Center marks a shift from weeks of waiting to same-day or next-day solutions. This advanced technology takes the digital blueprints created in our design software and translates them into tangible, biocompatible dental components layer by microscopic layer.

Whether we are producing surgical templates for guided implant placement or temporary crowns to protect your smile, the "additive" process ensures incredible material efficiency and structural integrity. By bringing this manufacturing capability in-house, we maintain total quality control over every device we deliver, ensuring that the physical result is a carbon copy of our digital perfection.

## Precision Manufacturing for the Modern Patient

When you undergo a procedure at our Murphy, NC practice, the Shining 3D printer works behind the scenes to streamline your clinical experience. Instead of dealing with the logistical delays of external laboratories, we can rapidly iterate and fabricate custom oral appliances such as nightguards, splints, or orthodontic models with sub-millimeter accuracy.

The high-resolution resin used in our printer is specifically selected for its durability and oral safety, providing a fit that is far more comfortable than mass-produced alternatives. This technology allows Dr. Bulsara to refine your treatment on the fly, offering a high-tech, efficient workflow that respects your time while providing world-class dental craftsmanship.
