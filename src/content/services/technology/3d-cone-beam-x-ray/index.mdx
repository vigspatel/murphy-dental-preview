## A Volumetric Leap Beyond Traditional Imaging

While standard dental X-rays provide a flat, two-dimensional view, the 3D Cone Beam (CBCT) scanner captures a complete volumetric reconstruction of your facial anatomy. During a quick, non-invasive scan, the machine rotates around your head, collecting hundreds of high-resolution images that are stitched together into a single 3D model.

This allows Dr. Bulsara to see the exact thickness of your jawbone, the precise pathway of sensitive nerves, and the hidden structures of your sinuses. By uncovering what lies beneath the surface in three dimensions, we can diagnose issues that traditional X-rays might miss, ensuring that your treatment plan is built on a foundation of absolute clinical certainty.

## Precision Diagnostics for Safer Procedures

When you visit Murphy Dental Center for a consultation, our CBCT technology serves as the "GPS" for your dental care. It is an indispensable tool for complex procedures like dental implant placement, wisdom tooth removal, and endodontic therapy, as it allows us to virtually perform your surgery on-screen before we ever begin the actual treatment.

This level of foresight minimizes surgical time, reduces the risk of complications, and allows for more conservative, less invasive techniques. Despite its power, the system is designed with patient safety in mind, utilizing "low-dose" technology that provides significantly more information than a standard X-ray while keeping radiation exposure to a minimum.
