## Magnification as a Diagnostic Powerhouse

Modern dentistry is often a game of millimeters, and the dental operating microscope provides an unparalleled view of the oral environment. By magnifying the treatment area up to 20 times and illuminating it with intense, shadow-free fiber-optic light, we can detect micro-fractures, hidden accessory canals, and early-stage decay that the naked eye simply cannot see.

This level of optical clarity is a game-changer for complex endodontic procedures and restorative work, ensuring that every intervention is based on a total understanding of your tooth's internal and external anatomy.

## Precision-Guided Care for Superior Outcomes

When you visit Murphy Dental Center, Dr. Bulsara utilizes the surgical microscope to perform "minimally invasive" dentistry with extreme confidence. Because we can see the tooth's structure in such vivid detail, we are able to preserve more of your healthy natural enamel and target only the compromised areas.

This precision reduces trauma to the surrounding tissues, leading to more predictable healing and highly durable long-term results. Our commitment to using this sophisticated visual technology in our Murphy, NC office ensures that your treatment is as conservative, accurate, and successful as possible.
